Trading Bot

Project 1

This is a cryptocurrency trading bot I coded in python using the Coinbase API. While trading, I realized that I missed out on key price movements while I was away from the trading screen. So, I decided to code a bot that would automate my actions. The bot first sends a market buy order and sets a pre-determined sell price below the current price. If the price moves up, the sell price follows it. If it moves down, the sell price doesn't move. Once the sell price is reached, a market sell order is sent and a buy price is set close to the sell price. If the price moves down, the buy price moves down and if the price moves up, the buy price doesn't move. There is a maximum "gap" between the current price and the buy price or the sell price that is pre-determined.
