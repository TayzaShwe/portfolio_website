Trading Bot

Project 1 (Independent Project)

This is a cryptocurrency trading bot I coded in python. I decided to code a bot that would automate my actions even when I was away from the trading screen. The bot maintains one sell price or one buy price at a given time. The difference between the current price and the buy/sell price never exceeds a pre-determined "gap". The bot's performance compared to the the simple buy-and-hold strategy is mixed. It does well in markets with large movements and does poorly in sideways markets. The loss incurred when executing a market order also plays a signficant role. There is a version that uses actual money and one that uses paper money (fake money). The technologies involved include Python, Matplotlib, Coinbase Pro API, and Cryptocompare API.
